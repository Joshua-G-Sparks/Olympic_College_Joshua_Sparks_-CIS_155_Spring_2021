Determining damage in GURPS

5/18/2021 9:52pm, Joshua Sparks

In GURPS, dealing damage to an enemy with a melee weapon is determined by your character's strength. Higher strength allows a character to lift more, endure more damage, and hit harder. Below is a chart that shows the relation between a character's Strength statistic and the damage they deal.
